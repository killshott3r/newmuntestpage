What is NewMUN

NewMUN is an interactive platform for students to research on topics, debate on an issue, and learn how to make solutions for the today's problems.

Research

NewMUN requires active research on the delegates' part to an issue given among the council and gather points to show the validity of a preferred solution.

Discussion

Facts are brought together or in opposition of other facts by other delegates to come to a conclusion in the NewMUN Council

Resolution

Delegates get the cance to form solutions for the Issues in their Council, just like a UN Council takes for the World

Model United Nations, for students!

An MUN conference is a great experience and a must-be-explored event.

  • Meet people and places – Get to know different nations and widen your friends circle
  • Public speaking – Have a fear facing audience? MUN conferences are the best in helping anyone and everyone overcome their fear of public speaking
  • Become acquainted with global issues – Your opinion may contribute to solve the problems that affect our planet today
  • Diplomacy and negotiation skill – MUNs are diplomatic simulations; respect and co-operation are fundamentals of the debate, helping us build our individuality

Why NewMUN?

NewMUN is one of the best organized model united nations in the world. Here's why.

By the Students

NewMUN is as democratic as all UN Institutions. Right from Organizing, Chairing, Planning, Video Editing, Graphic Designing, Web Hosting is completely done by Students.

Of the Students

NewMUN has students representing countries or people and fight for their issues and concerns, and come to a solution.

For the Students

NewMUN is organized for all students, from Grade 7 to Grade 12.

Prince Prakash
Prince Prakash is a Grade 11, Science student of The New Indian School. His first MUN experience was in his 7th grade and he was hesitant in the initial parts of the conference. But as soon as he participated in his first Challenge against a senior upon Chair’s discretion, he got all worked up. He later bagged the ‘Best Speaker’ award in the same conference. Ever since, he has found MUNs as the right platform to bring out the best among students in terms of thinking, debating and discussing. He also feels that every student should take part in MUNs, as it develops them to think on a global level and find solutions to real world problems. He also believes that a MUN experience will help delegates to unleash the potential they never knew they had. His other interests include cricket, basketball, chess, dancing, scientific theories and enjoying the art of films. Being the Secretary General for the first time, he looks forward to meet all of you and expects the delegates to attend the two-day conference with zeal and fervor.
Alina Mahesh
Alina Mahesh, currently studying in XIth grade of the science stream, will be chairing the International Press as well as heading the Finance Team. Having participated in 2 MUNs, she has earned the title of the ‘Most Diplomatic Speaker’ twice. Besides this, she has also participated in other intra as well as inter school competitions. She believes that MUNs provide children with the opportunity to widen their spectrum of knowledge on various matters. She believes that MUNs possess something magical that helps in transforming and illuminating a student’s life. Being the Head of Journalists, Alina believes that restricting journalism is equivalent to restricting democracy. A transparent and free press would be the perfect components of democracy. According to Alina, MUN is not just a platform where you come, speak and go, but, it helps in molding and shaping the young diplomats. As Malala Yousafzai once quoted, “I raise up my voice — not so that I can shout, but so that those without a voice can be heard.” Alina expects each and every delegate to rise and be the voice of the world. The world looks up to the upcoming generation for a new era of hope. So keep moving on the track of confidence and never let your fire of hope extinguish out.
